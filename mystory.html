My Story

EDUCATION
I'm now a doctoral student and a graduate teaching assistant in Communication at Michigan State University. I earned a BA and MA in the discipline at Nanjing University, China. 

WHO I AM AND WHAT I AM DOING
I'm a first-generation college student who came from a small motor town in China to another motor town('s neighboring city). My childhood idol Madonna was also born in Michigan. So hey it seems the dream came true. You must be my lucky star!

Growing up in the working class community where I was surrounded by all the cool big locomotives and gradually developed my interests in mechanics and technical stuff. Therefore, it came naturally that I largely picked up some quantitative analysis and computational skills by myself because I think it much fun to play with all the technical stuff! But then the question is, what do we study?

These days, I'm studying artificial intelligence(AI)-driven persuasion.

AI has been widely applied to different aspects of our daily life--health, dating, news, commerce, etc. To some extent, it is believed by a lot of people to be able to make their own decision, just like us humans, which is quite different from other technologies we have seen. It is challenging our knowledge about human-technology relations yet we don't know enough about them and about their social impacts yet.

Researchers have been working in the direction and I'm joining them but I'm doing more than that. Apart from studying human-AI relation, I want to capitalize research findings and apply them to persuasion research. That is, I'm interested in how we can leverage AI for well-intended persuasion, such as persuading people to adopt healthier lifestyles.
