"Would you listen to me?"

Research Interest

Previously, I studied health persuasion and computer-mediated communication. 

These days, I'm working on artificial intelligence(AI)-driven persuasion.

AI has been widely applied to different aspects of our daily life--health, dating, news, commerce, etc. To some extent, it is believed by a lot of people to be able to make their own decision, just like us humans, which is quite different from other technologies we have seen. It is challenging our knowledge about human-technology relations yet we don't know enough about them and about their social impacts yet.

Researchers have been working in the direction and I'm joining them but I'm doing more than that. Apart from studying human-AI relation, I want to capitalize research findings and apply them to persuasion research. That is, I'm interested in how we can leverage AI for well-intended persuasion, such as persuading people to adopt healthier lifestyles.
